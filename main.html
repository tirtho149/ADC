About Us

Apon Dental Care is a state-of-the-art dental clinic dedicated to providing comprehensive and compassionate dental care to our community. With a team of experienced dentists and cutting-edge technology, we strive to enhance the oral health and smiles of our patients.

Our Services

General Dentistry

Our general dentistry services cover routine check-ups, cleanings, and preventive care. We focus on early detection and treatment of dental issues to maintain optimal oral health.

Orthodontics

We offer orthodontic treatments to help you achieve a straight and beautiful smile. Our orthodontic specialists provide personalized treatment plans, including braces and Invisalign.

Oral Surgery

Our oral surgery procedures are conducted by experienced and skilled surgeons. From tooth extraction to more complex surgeries, we prioritize patient comfort and safety.
